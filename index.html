Our Story

Crafting Beautiful Homes Since 2010

Care & Arts was founded with a simple belief: everyone deserves a beautifully furnished home without the overwhelming experience of traditional furniture shopping.

What started as a single curated showroom in downtown San Francisco has grown into a destination for design lovers across the country. We partner with over 50 of the world's finest furniture brands, carefully selecting pieces that balance timeless design with everyday functionality.

Our dedicated team of interior designers and furniture specialists are here to guide you through every decision, whether you are furnishing a studio apartment or a five-bedroom home.
